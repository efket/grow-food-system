Who we are

Data controller: Efket Ltd, trading as Grow Food System.

What data we collect and why

We process your data on the basis of consent. You give this by completing the sign-up form. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email we send you.

How we use your data

We use your email address to:

  • Send the free guide you requested

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Email marketing service

We use MailerLite to manage our email list and send emails. MailerLite acts as a data processor on our behalf and is GDPR-compliant. You can read their privacy policy at mailerlite.com.

How long we keep your data

We retain your data for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your email address is removed from our active list. You can request full deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time — use the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data has been mishandled

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@growfoodsystem.com.

Cookies and analytics

This website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for analytics and advertising measurement. Specifically:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site (pages visited, time on site, traffic sources). Google Analytics sets cookies to collect this information. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • Google Ads conversion tracking — we use Google Ads to run paid advertising. A conversion tracking tag fires when a visitor completes a sign-up, allowing us to measure the effectiveness of our ads. This uses cookies set by Google.

These cookies are set by Google and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can manage or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings.
